# Paper Library Instructions

- Keep primary game evidence, the project whitepaper, and research references
  in their existing subdirectories.
- Record new sources in `CATALOG.md` and cite PDF page numbers.
- Add rule-bearing source excerpts only to `game-sources/EVIDENCE.md`, with a
  stable `evidence:` marker and minimal transcription.
- Do not put experiment reports, generated logs, or solver status in `papers/`.
- Re-render and visually inspect the whitepaper PDF after changing its TeX.
